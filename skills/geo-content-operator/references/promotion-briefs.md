## Seed Briefs

### AI Search Optimization For B2B SaaS

- target keyword: AI search optimization
- business objective: educate B2B SaaS teams on how to make content recommendation-ready for AI answers
- audience: founders, growth leaders, content leaders
- product angle: a GEO tool should identify missing recommendation context, evidence gaps, and page-level fixes, not only report mentions
- proof available: user-provided business context only; no customer proof supplied
- content lane: category authority with ambient product logic
- mention intensity: ambient or comparative
- required asset: recommendation-contract framework or diagnostic checklist
- avoid: fake benchmark data, fake customer examples, generic SEO-vs-GEO explainer

### AI Overviews And Citation Readiness

- target keyword: AI Overviews optimization
- business objective: connect AI search behavior to concrete page improvements
- audience: content and growth teams
- product angle: identify which pages are citation-ready and which need evidence blocks
- proof available: requires live research or user-supplied examples
- content lane: repair or product-led proof
- mention intensity: comparative
- required asset: citation-readiness checklist
- avoid: claiming ranking factors without sources
